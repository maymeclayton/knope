About Leslie
For years, Leslie Knope has served the city of Pawnee, Indiana as Deputy Director of the Parks Department. She, her husband, Ben, and their triplets moved to Lexington 3 years ago. She enjoys work, waffles, and talking on the phone approximately 8 times a day with her best friend, Ann.

Since moving to the great city of Lexington she has felt compelled to serve. She truly believes that government is by the people for the people. Her experience with the Parks Department in Pawnee and eagerness to serve make her the best candidate for the position of Constable.
